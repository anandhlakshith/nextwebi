Steps to Change Your Offshore Developer
1. Review Your Contract
  • Check for clauses related to termination, notice period, and intellectual property.
  • Ensure you retain rights to all code, documentation, and assets.
2. Document Everything
  • Backup all source code, credentials, and project documentation.
  • Create a clear handover checklist to avoid knowledge loss.
Choose a New Offshore Partner
  • Evaluate new vendors based on:
    • Technical expertise
    • Communication skills

4. Plan the Transition
  • Run a short overlap period between old and new teams.

5. Ensure Continuity
  • Use version control (e.g., Git) and CI/CD pipelines to maintain code integrity.
  • Maintain regular check-ins and performance reviews with the new team.
